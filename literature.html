Why it's art?

We can tell that Architecture is art for the reason that building does not only cost sweat & strength, it comes from the most primal instincts, the need of seeking shelter, from the fear of getting eaten by the wild creatures out there, from the imminent chance of dying, it's a way of preserving our species, it all comes from a need in conjunction with creativity & imagination, all of this comes from within, from the core, from our soul, we extract ideas, carve them into different kinds of materials and pile them to form structures that have the purpose of sheltering us.

What happens when we have fulfilled our necessity?

We find peace & calm, peace gifts us the opportunity to look deep inside, to glimpse at our consciousness, that could be an uncomfortable place, sometimes it's not. Our emotions and ideas merge into creative power, now we need to express it, it always finds a way out.

In some cases these structures where built to worship some mystic deity.
